## About Windrose

**Windrose** is a survival adventure set in an alternative Age of Piracy. It combines the classic "build, craft, survive" formula with intense melee combat and engaging exploration — both on land and at sea. Sail the open ocean, discover uncharted islands, build your pirate stronghold, and fend off rival crews and sea monsters in this swashbuckling multiplayer survival experience.
