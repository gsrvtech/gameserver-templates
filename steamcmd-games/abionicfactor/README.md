## About Abiotic Factor

**Abiotic Factor** is a survival crafting game for 1–6 players set in the depths of a secret underground research facility. Caught between a paranormal containment failure, a military crusade, and chaos spilling in from a dozen dimensions, the world's greatest minds must band together and survive against the universe's greatest threats. Scavenge, craft, build, and fight your way through the sprawling complex — alone or with friends.
