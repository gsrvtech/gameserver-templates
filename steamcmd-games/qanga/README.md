## About QANGA

**QANGA** is a vast open-world space sandbox where you can explore the entire solar system without a single loading screen. Fly freely between planets, land seamlessly on Mars, Venus, the Moon, Jupiter's moons, Saturn's rings, and countless orbital stations. Every planet and moon is an open playground to explore and exploit as you see fit. Craft your own adventure solo or with friends, create your own game servers, and experiment with alternative gameplay modes — all in one seamlessly connected universe.
